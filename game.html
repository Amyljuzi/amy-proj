Identity V - The Basics

About the Game

Identity V is an asymmetrical horror mobile game developed by NetEase. Players either take the role of Survivors attempting to escape or the Hunter trying to capture them. The game features a gothic, Victorian setting, where strategy, teamwork, and stealth are key elements of survival.

Survivors

Survivors must work together to decode cipher machines and escape while avoiding the Hunter. Each survivor has unique abilities that can support the team.

  • Doctor

    The Doctor can heal herself and others quickly. Her healing abilities make her a key asset to a team of survivors.

  • Lawyer

    The Lawyer can use a special ability to manipulate the environment and reduce the Hunter's ability to track.

  • Thief

    The Thief excels at evasion and has skills that help in stealing important items for other survivors.

  • Gardener

    The Gardener can use her unique abilities to heal and protect survivors, especially during the escape phase.

  • Coordinator

    The Coordinator is an expert in crowd control, with skills to stun the Hunter for a short time to help others escape.

  • Mechanic

    The Mechanic is able to repair cipher machines quickly and has a robot companion that can help with decoding and other tasks.

Hunters

Hunters aim to capture all Survivors before they can escape. Their unique abilities make them dangerous predators, constantly lurking in the shadows.

  • Jack (The Ripper)

    Jack (The Ripper) specializes in quick, deadly attacks and can teleport to survivors in a flash, making him a terrifying opponent.

  • Leo (Hell Ember)

    Leo (Hell Ember) can summon flames to damage survivors and has high mobility to track and hunt them down efficiently.

  • Bane (Gamekeeper)

    Bane (Gamekeeper) uses traps to hinder survivors' movement and can create barriers to isolate them from the rest of the group.

  • Violetta (Soul Weaver)

    Violetta (Soul Weaver) uses her ability to create soul-chains to track and attack survivors from a distance.

  • Joker (Smiley Face)

    Joker (Smiley Face) utilizes a deadly trickery-based attack and can disorient survivors with illusions and traps.
